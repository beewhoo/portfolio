ABOUT

Hi, I'm Bibek, an enthusiastic web developer with an interest in UX/UI design. I am a former account manager, who decided to trust his instincts and pursue a passion for web development. During my tenure in sales, I had the opportunity to work with many wonderful developers and designers. This is where my appreciation for development first started. I quit my job and enrolled in coding bootcamp at Bitmaker Lab. In during this learning journey, I have proved myself to be a committed learner. The ability to constantly be learning and developing new skills is an important factor in my passion.

P.S. I love almost anything under the sun, apart from Real Madrid and slow wifi.
